🛡️ SafeGuard – Women & Child Safety Platform
Overview

SafeGuard is an AI-powered Women and Child Safety Platform designed to provide immediate assistance during emergencies through intelligent threat detection, automated SOS alerts, location tracking, volunteer support, and law enforcement integration.

The platform aims to reduce emergency response time by combining real-time monitoring, voice recognition, risk zone analysis, and community-driven safety features.

✨ Core Features
🚨 Emergency SOS System
One-tap SOS activation
Automatic emergency alert generation
Sends distress notifications to guardians and emergency contacts
Shares real-time GPS location
🎙️ Voice Recognition-Based Threat Detection
Detects predefined distress keywords and phrases
Can trigger SOS automatically when the user cannot access the device manually
Works continuously in background monitoring mode (with user consent)
📍 Risk Zone Detection
Identifies potentially unsafe areas
Generates risk scores for locations
Alerts users when entering high-risk zones
Supports future machine learning-based danger prediction
👤 User Profile System
Personal information management
Emergency contact registration
Safety preferences customization
Medical and identification information storage
🤝 Volunteer Network
Registered volunteers can assist nearby users during emergencies
Volunteer profiles with verification status
Location-aware emergency notifications
👮 Police Dashboard
Dedicated dashboard for law enforcement
View active emergency reports
Access victim location and incident details
Manage emergency response workflow
🗺️ Alternate Safe Route Navigation
Suggests safer routes instead of only shortest routes
Avoids identified high-risk areas
Dynamic route recommendations
🧠 AI Incident Analysis
AI-generated summaries of emergency incidents
Faster understanding of reported situations
Supports emergency responders and authorities
📸 Image-to-Text Processing
Extracts text from uploaded images
Useful for:
Vehicle numbers
Addresses
Documents
Incident-related evidence
🔒 Authentication & Security
Secure login system powered by Clerk
User authentication and authorization
Protected routes and role-based access
